ABOUT KATARIA

Kataria Transport Company is a leading goods transportation service provider operating across India. Built on a culture of openness, trust and free communication, we have established ourselves as the most dependable logistics partner for India's most respected organizations.

Our merit-based career growth framework fosters talent and dedication, enabling us to deliver excellence in every shipment. With 106+ multi-axle vehicles and 24 own branches, we provide unmatched Pan India coverage.
